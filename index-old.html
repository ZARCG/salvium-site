About Salvium

Salvium is taking a 'privacy-first' approach while striving to fully comply with the Markets in Crypto-Assets (MiCA) regulations. Built on a fork of Monero, it retains advanced privacy features such as stealth addresses and ring signatures while introducing groundbreaking innovations in transaction mechanics.
